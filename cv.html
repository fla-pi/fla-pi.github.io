Research projects

  • Adopt a Construction - Towards an Italian Constructicon
    April 2022 - Ongoing
    Project board member (with L. Busso e M. S. Micheli) for the project Adopt a Construction, at the Department of Modern Languages, Literatures and Cultures (LILEC) of the University of Bologna.
    The project, under the scientific direction of professor Francesca Masini, aims at the creation of the first Italian Constructicon as a collaborative resource.

  • DILLo - Database Italiano del Lessico per Logopedisti
    June 2021 - Ongoing
    Collaborator in the realization of DILLo (Italian Database of Lexicon for Speech Terapists), an online lexical database of Italian, designed for speech therapists' needs.
    Tasks: Selection of diatopic variants of the lexemes, phonetic and phonemic transcription, revision of the database.
